# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors and community members, regardless of background or identity.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments
- Public or private attacks on others
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Enforcement

Community members who violate this code may be temporarily or permanently banned from community spaces.

To report violations, contact the maintainers privately at maintainers@cardano-flutter-sdk.dev.
